About Us

Local, Reliable, Hardworking

Chesapeake Junk and Hauling LLC is a locally owned and operated junk removal company serving Cecil County and Harford County, Maryland. Owner and operator Tyler built this business on one simple idea: show up fast, work hard, and charge fair prices.

Whether you need a single piece of furniture hauled away or a full property cleanout, we treat every job with the same care and professionalism. We're your neighbors — not a franchise.
